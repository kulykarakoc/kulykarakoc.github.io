About Me.

Web Developer. Writer. Educator.

I am a former English teacher who is new to the tech industry. Having taught English literature and language to high school students in Texas, Qatar, Turkey (and then Texas, again!), I do not shy away from adaptation. When life changed in March 2020, I reflected, as all of us did, on what I had and had not yet done in my life.

By April 2021, I decided it was time for a bold change. I enrolled in a coding certification course with MIT. Two months later, I resigned from my teaching job and decided to fully pursue coding. Having successfully taught hybrid the whole year, I realized there is great potential to collaborate and create on the internet. I wanted to know the inner workings of what we ALL live and breathe: the web.

I am a dedicated mom of two beautiful children, and I adore the fulfillment working hard with like-minded peers affords me. I am a believer that with the right support and the right drive, a woman can have it all!
